Production pack · 12 reels · 9:16

AquaDealers USP Video Pack

One video per USP. Each card is a complete shoot plan: the hook, scene-by-scene storyboard with timecodes, on-screen overlay text in English + Telugu, voiceover script, emotion arc, music, props, and the closing CTA. Shoot everything on a phone at the shop — real counter, real farmers, real feed bags.

Ground rules for every video

  • Format: Vertical 9:16, 1080×1920, 30fps. Length 25–35 seconds. Also export a 15s cut (hook + best scene + CTA) for ads.
  • First 3 seconds decide everything. Open on the pain or the wow — never on a logo.
  • Language: Voiceover in Telugu, overlays bilingual (Telugu big, English small). Always burn in subtitles — most reels are watched on mute.
  • Real people, real shop. A dealer in a lungi at his own counter beats an actor. Ask 2–3 friendly farmers for 10 minutes on camera.
  • Phone screen shots: record the app with built-in screen recording (clean status bar, 100% battery icon hidden if possible), then film the phone in-hand for context shots. Never mix the two in one cut.
  • Light: shoot morning 7–10am at the counter facing the entrance light. No tube-light-behind-head shots.
  • End card (same for all 12): logo, one line "మీ షాప్ కోసం తయారైన యాప్" (An app built for your shop), phone number + "WhatsApp చేయండి", 2s hold.
  • Music: use trending Telugu reel audio when posting organically (reach), switch to the listed royalty-free mood for paid ads.
